Hi Keith,

I recently read your blog (keithtenzer.com), and enjoyed your perspective on software deployment automation.

I’m reaching out to invite you to join us as panelist in one of our upcoming Continuous Discussions episodes, to share your experiences in Agile, Devops, Continuous Delivery and related topics.

*Continuous Discussions* is an open forum to discuss topics related to Agile, DevOps and Continuous Delivery. The online panel is hosted by Electric Cloud - we power Continuous Delivery at businesses like SpaceX, Cisco, GE and E*TRADE by automating their build, test and deployment processes.
