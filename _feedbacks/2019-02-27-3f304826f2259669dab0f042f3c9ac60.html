Hi Keith,
We are hiring an Senior Solutions Engineer who is responsible for providing pre- and post- technical sales support in EMEA region for Enterprise accounts and Partners. The candidate will also be performing consulting & training tasks in the region as required. The candidate must have fluent written and verbal German language skills. Our product is Open Source database.
The compensation structure is a base salary and a variable component based on revenues and quarterly objectives.
RESPONSIBILITIES
• Support pre-sales activities in the designated territory(ies) and region by:
• Participating in sales calls and onsite visits with prospects to make technical presentations
• Participate / present at industry shows, user groups and other public events or webinars
• Preparing appropriate roadmap presentations for key accounts
• Working with Product Managers to provide input to the roadmap and create the customer ready presentations
• Track, monitor, and respond to company interactions with existing customers by:
• Ensuring the technical win for each sales opportunity
• Running workshops and demos to evaluate new technologies
• Follow Tech Support incidents to help overcome roadblocks
• Document customers’ product wish list
• Mine accounts to assist the Sales Representatives grow the account
• Collaborate with Services delivery team for onsite visits to key accounts in the territory
• Interact with Product Management and Engineering teams to help steer the products based on customer demands and prospect trends:
• Acting as the customer advocate
• Help define product features participating in key product meetings as needed
• Validate new service and product offerings with customers and prospects.
• Interact with Channel and other Go To Market Partners
REQUIREMENTS
• B.A. / B.Sc. or equivalent work experience.
• 5 years track record in software solution pre-sales, with database experience highly preferred.
• Knowledge of one or more of the following database technologies
• NoSQL – MongoDB, Redis, Apache Cassandra
• SQL – Oracle, Sybase, DB2, SQL Server
• Knowledge of database design, administration, and architecture
• Knowledge of multiple programming languages (developer experience recommended):
• PHP, Java, C, C#, Python
• Experience with Web/Application environments recommended:
• Apache, Tomcat, JBoss, Weblogic, Websphere, Lighthttd
• Sysadmin experience from the following operating systems:
• Linux RedHat/CentOS and Debian/Ubuntu
• Windows 2000 and 2003 server
• Familiarity of systems installation, configuration, monitoring & management
• Ansible, Salt, Chef, Puppet
• Zabbix, Cacti, Nagios, MonYOG, Prometheus
• NewRelic, AppDynamics
• Experience with virtualization & containerization software required:
• Xen, Virtualbox, Vmware
• Docker
• MesoSphere, Kubernetes
• Amazon EC2 and other cloud knowledge recommended
• Very strong troubleshooting and problem solving skills
• Team player with excellent verbal and written communication skills.
• Ability to travel as required.
• Ability to work remotely.
• Excellent command of the German language (written and spoken)
• Excellent spoken and written English is a requirement. Multiple languages and/or multi-country experience is a plus.
LOCATION
Candidate must be located in Germany or an EU country.

Best,
