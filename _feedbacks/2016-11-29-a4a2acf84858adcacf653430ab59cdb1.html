Hi Keith,

My name is David and I’m responsible for community initiatives at Stratoscale. I’m writing to let you know we recently launched our Private Cloud Wiki, a vendor-agnostic compilation of thousands of the best expert resources on private cloud infrastructure, virtualization and hyperconvergence.

Your site, keithtenzer.com, is included under the topic ‘OpenStack Bare Metal Provisioning (Ironic)’ (see the section ‘How To’):
http://wiki.stratoscale.com/display/privatecloud/OpenStack+Bare+Metal+Provisioning+-+Ironic

Would you like us to make any changes to your listing on this page? Or do you have any other content you’d like to suggest for one of our topics?
